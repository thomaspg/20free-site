20free — Privacy Policy

Our games collect no data. None.

This policy applies to all mobile games published by 20free.

  • No account. Our games require no registration, no email, no login.
  • No data collection. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage data, or device data. There are no analytics, no trackers, no third-party SDKs collecting anything.
  • No ads. Our games contain no advertising of any kind.
  • Offline. Your game progress is stored only on your device. The only network request our games make is to display the list of other 20free games.
  • Purchases. The one-time unlock purchase is processed entirely by Google Play (or the App Store). We never see or store your payment details. Refer to your store's privacy policy for the transaction itself.
